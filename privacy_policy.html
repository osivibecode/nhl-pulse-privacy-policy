🏒 NHL Pulse Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 19, 2026

1. Overview

osivibecode operates the US Hockey Pulse (NHL Pulse) mobile application. This Privacy Policy outlines how your data is handled. We are committed to transparency and respect your privacy. In short: we do not collect personally identifiable information (PII), and all your user preferences are stored directly on your device.

2. Data Stored Locally

The app uses Android DataStore Preferences and iOS local storage to save your settings. This data is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Favorite Teams & Bookmakers: Your selections used to filter scores, standings, and betting odds.
  • Notification Settings: Preferences for goals, period changes, and game alerts.
  • App Preferences: Your selected theme, locale (12 languages supported), and HUD/layout sizes.
  • Prediction History: A record of games you have predicted within the app.
  • Technical Sync Logs: Snapshots used to ensure live data (like scores) is refreshed efficiently.

3. Third-Party Services

To provide real-time data and keep the app free, we use the following vetted services:

  • Google AdMob: Displays advertisements. AdMob collects Identifiers (like your device's advertising ID) and Usage Data (ad interactions) to serve and measure ads.
  • Google Firebase (Cloud Messaging & Firestore): Used for delivering instant goal notifications and fetching the latest odds/rosters. Firebase processes anonymous installation identifiers to route messages correctly.
  • NHL API: The source for real-time scores, stats, and standings. No user data is sent to this API.

4. App Tracking Transparency (iOS Only)

On iOS devices, NHL Pulse utilizes Apple’s App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework.

  • Granting Permission: If you allow tracking, the app accesses your device’s Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA) to show you more relevant and personalized ads via Google AdMob.
  • Declining Permission: If you decline, the app functions exactly the same, but ads will be generic.
  • Control: You can toggle this setting at any time in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.

5. Required Permissions

  • Internet: Required to fetch live hockey data.
  • Notifications: Required to alert you about game events (user-optional).
  • Foreground Service: Ensures live scoreboard updates remain reliable while the app is active.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Because your data is stored locally, we do not have access to it. You can delete all your data at any time by:

  1. Clearing the app's cache and storage in your device settings.
  2. Uninstalling the application.
